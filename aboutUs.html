About Us

Here at Lorraine’s A Taste of the South we create homecooked meals made from recipes that were passed down from generations.

From Mama's Meatloaf, to the Louisiana Style Collards and Candied Sweets! Add to that the Honey Buttered Cornbread, Homemade Sweet Tea and Southern Desserts!

We hope to make your plate dance and warm your hearts as we bring the soul of the south from our family to yours. Blessings, Louis and Lorraine
